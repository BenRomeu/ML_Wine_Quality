Introduction

The database is composed of two datasets which are related to red and white variants of the Portuguese "Vinho Verde" wine.

For our analysis we focused exclusively our attention on the white wine dataset.

The goal is to model wine quality based on physicochemical tests. For our experiment we divided quality (a range from 1 to 9) into a binary factor: Good & Bad, following the rule : Good >= 6.

Initially we have chosen to assign the Good label to wines whose quality was superior or equal to 7. But due to non-significant results, we re-evaluated our decision with the consequence that we there are now more Good wines then Bad ones.
